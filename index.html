Country Visit Map

Upload your Google Takeout Timeline.json or a CSV with country visits to see a map of countries you've visited. Upload both to combine them. All processing happens in your browser — your data is not uploaded anywhere. NB Coded by AI with Claude Code.

How to use this tool
  1. Get your data: Open Google Maps on your phone, go to Settings > Personal content > Export Timeline data. Or prepare a CSV with "Date" and "Country" columns.
  2. Upload: Drop your Timeline.json and/or CSV file below, or click to select. You can upload both at once to combine them.
  3. View: A world map appears showing every country you've visited, colored by how recently you were there.
