Country Visit Map

Upload your Google Takeout Timeline.json to see a map of countries you've visited. All processing happens in your browser — your data is not uploaded anywhere. NB Coded by AI with Claude Code.

How to use this tool
  1. Get your data: Open Google Maps on your phone, go to Settings > Personal content > Export Timeline data. Download the resulting file.
  2. Upload: Drop the Timeline.json file below or click to select it.
  3. View: A world map appears showing every country you've visited, colored by how recently you were there.
